Practical tools you can use today

  1. Set a deposit limit in your casino account settings before you start playing.
  2. Use reality checks or session time reminders if your casino offers them.
  3. Self-exclude from a specific casino, or nationally via BetStop, if you need a break.
  4. Track your spending separately from your regular budget, and stop if it stops being fun.
