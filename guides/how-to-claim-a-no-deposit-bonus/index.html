Common mistakes that delay a bonus

What goes wrong

  • Using a name or address that doesn't match your ID
  • Missing or ignoring the verification email
  • Playing a game the spins aren't loaded onto
  • Assuming winnings are withdrawable before wagering clears

How to avoid it

  • Enter your details carefully and consistently at sign-up
  • Check spam/junk folders if the email doesn't arrive within a few minutes
  • Confirm the eligible game in the bonus terms before playing
  • Track your wagering progress in your account dashboard

Editorial note: the single biggest cause of a "missing" bonus we hear about is an unverified email — check that step first before contacting support.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to enter payment details to claim a no deposit bonus?

No — that's the defining feature of a no deposit bonus. You may be asked for payment details later, only if and when you choose to deposit.

What if my bonus doesn't appear?

Verify your email first, then check the bonus tab in your account. If it's still missing, contact live support with your registration email and the code used.

Can I claim at both Bitkingz and Crocoslots?

Yes — they're separate operators, so claiming at one has no bearing on your eligibility at the other.
