App or mobile browser?

Neither Bitkingz nor Crocoslots requires a downloaded app — both run fully in a mobile browser via responsive, touch-optimised sites, which is also how most Australian no deposit casinos operate today. Tips for a smoother mobile sign-up

Do

  • Check your email app immediately after registering — verification is usually instant
  • Use your phone's autofill carefully, then double-check your name and address match your ID
  • Bookmark the casino's mobile site for quick access to your bonus balance

Avoid

  • Registering over unstable public Wi-Fi mid-verification
  • Using a temporary or work email your phone can't check later
  • Assuming a "download our app" prompt from a third-party site is official — only use the casino's own domain
