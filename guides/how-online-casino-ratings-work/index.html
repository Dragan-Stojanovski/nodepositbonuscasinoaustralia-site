Our testing process

  1. Register a real account at the casino, as any player would.
  2. Claim and use the bonus being reviewed, tracking every step and timing.
  3. Test support with real questions via live chat or email.
  4. Complete KYC and request a withdrawal, timing the full payout process.
  5. Score each category based on what we actually experienced, not marketing claims.
